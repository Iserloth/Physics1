AI Usage

AI tools helped build this LMS. I use AI to:

  • Create the cinematic art and icons.
  • Generate podcast scripts for lessons.
  • Prototype interactive simulations and virtual apps.
  • Keep notes and layouts consistent from unit to unit.

My advice about AI

Using AI well is more than copy/paste. Train it with specifics and make sure you understand the results.

  • Use it for brainstorming and getting unstuck.
  • You are responsible for understanding your final work.
  • Submitting work you don’t understand is plagiarism and has consequences.
